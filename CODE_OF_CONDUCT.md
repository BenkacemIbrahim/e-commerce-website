# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions
- Provide technical feedback with clear reasoning

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through private channels and include any relevant context.
